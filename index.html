Overview

"Dominion" is a popular deck-building card game designed by Donald X. Vaccarino and first published in 2008. It is often credited with popularizing the deck-building genre, where players construct their decks during the game itself rather than bringing a pre-constructed deck to the game.

Phases of the Game

Action

Players can play one Action card from their hand, which might grant them special abilities, extra actions, or extra card draws

Buy

Players can spend their coins (from treasure cards played) to buy new cards from the supply.

Clean-up

Players place all played cards and any unspent coins into their discard pile. Then, they draw a new hand of 5 cards.
